SUPPORT & FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers! Check out our frequently asked questions below.

Simply click the "Play Now" button on our homepage! SUMO QUBES runs directly in your mobile browser - no download required. Just make sure you have a stable internet connection and you're ready to jump into the action!

SUMO QUBES is optimized for mobile devices including smartphones and tablets. The game works on both iOS and Android devices through your mobile browser. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome, Safari, or Firefox on a device with at least 2GB of RAM.

Yes! SUMO QUBES is completely free to play. We may offer optional cosmetic items or the Fame Pass for players who want to customize their experience, but the core game is free for everyone.

The leaderboard tracks your performance based on wins, eliminations, and match placement. You can view both Global and Local leaderboards. Rankings update in real-time, and you can compete for the top spots each season. Check your rank on the homepage or in your player profile!

Seasons are time-limited gameplay periods, typically lasting 2-3 months. Each season brings new content, challenges, rewards, and leaderboard resets. You can check out previous seasons in our Season Archive!

If you encounter a bug, please email us at fun@rofl.ie with a detailed description and screenshots if possible. To report a cheater, use the in-game report function or contact our support team with the player's username and details of the incident. We take all reports seriously!

Yes! While the game currently supports solo matches, we're working on adding party features so you can team up with friends. Follow our news section and join our Discord for updates on new features!

The Fame Pass is a premium seasonal progression system that offers exclusive cosmetics, emotes, and rewards. It's completely optional - you can enjoy the full game without it. Check our Fame Pass page for more details on the current season's rewards!

First, check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. Make sure you're on a stable Wi-Fi or mobile data connection. If problems persist, try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser. Contact support if issues continue.

You can change your display name from your account settings once you're logged in. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, select "Settings," and look for the "Display Name" option. Note that name changes may have a cooldown period.

The game uses simple touch controls optimized for mobile. A tutorial appears the first time you play. You can also access control information in the settings menu during gameplay. The controls are intuitive and easy to learn!

Yes! You can unlock various skins, colors, and accessories as you progress through the game. Some cosmetics are earned through gameplay, while others can be obtained through the Fame Pass or special events.
