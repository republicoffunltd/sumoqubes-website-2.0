OUR STORY

Republic of Fun Limited was founded with a simple mission: create games that bring people together through pure, unadulterated fun.

SUMO QUBES was born from late-night brainstorming sessions where we asked ourselves: "What if sumo wrestling met Minecraft in a mobile browser game?" The result? A chaotic, colorful, cubic battle arena that's captured the hearts of players worldwide.

What started as a small project has grown into a thriving community of thousands of players. Every match, every tournament, every leaderboard climb adds to the story we're building together.

We're just getting started. Join us as we continue to expand the SUMO QUBES universe with new features, events, and surprises. This is YOUR game, YOUR community, YOUR arena.
